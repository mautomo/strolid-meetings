Brand Essence

Vandoko is a B2B market-intelligence platform for automotive dealers. The brand reads like a precision instrument: dark, focused, alive with data. Mission console, not marketing brochure.

Personality

Precise · Operational · Premium. We surface signal, not noise.

02

Logo & Brandmark

Five primary assets: the wordmark (white and black, transparent) for headers and brand moments, the VK brandmark with no background for compact spaces, and two app-icon tiles (dark and light). Marks are single color — white on dark, obsidian on light — never cyan, never two-tone.

Primary assets — 5 total

Captions sit outside each lockup · clear space ≥ the “K” counter on all sides · wordmark min 20px, brandmark min 16px.

Clear space & minimum size

Rules
  • Clear space ≥ the height of the “K” counter on all sides.
  • Wordmark min height 20px; brandmark min 16px.
  • Single color only — white on dark, obsidian on light. Never cyan, never two-tone.
  • Never stretch, rotate, add shadows, or reconstruct the letterforms.
✓ Do
  • Render both marks in the same single color (white on dark).
  • Use the v2 rounded-tile icon for app / avatar contexts.
  • Give the wordmark generous obsidian negative space.
✕ Don't
  • Tint the mark cyan or use the light-cyan fill in UI.
  • Mix colors between the wordmark and brandmark.
  • Place on busy photography without an obsidian scrim.

14

Icons & Motion

Iconography is lucide-react, 1.5–2px stroke, default size-4 (16px) inline with text. Motion is subtle, purposeful, and respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Icon rules
  • lucide-react only; match stroke weight to text.
  • Auto-sizes to size-4 inside buttons.
  • Tint with currentColor — cyan only for active/accent.
Motion scale
  • Micro 150ms · element 300ms · section 500ms · hero 800ms.
  • ease-out in, ease-in out — never linear UI.
  • Signatures: gauge tick stagger 12ms, segment stagger 42ms — no pulsing dots or spinning orbs.
  • prefers-reduced-motion collapses all to ~0.

Token discipline

Raw hex, inline hsl(), ad-hoc rgba() shadows, and white/N opacity ladders are forbidden inside components — they belong in tokens. New colors / spacing / fonts ship in standalone token PRs. A PreToolUse hook scans every .tsx/.html write for violations.
